Artists at Work Collective
Mission Statement

The Artists at Work Collective is the latest creativity incubator in New Mexico uniting diverse and unique artists in pursuit of collective sounds and projects. Not limited to one genre, "sound", or even art form, we work to incubate music at every level, finding unique and creative ways to use music/art as a vehicle of social and civic change. In so many words---we use music and the arts to change the world.
